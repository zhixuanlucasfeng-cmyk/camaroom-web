Inside Restar Solar

Our factory & production line

Restar Solar panels and storage products are built in our own ISO-9001 certified factory. From cell stringing and lamination to EL testing, framing and final quality control, every module is made and checked under one roof before it ships to Cameroon.

01

Cell stringing

High-efficiency cells are soldered into strings by automated welders.

02

Lay-up & lamination

Strings are laid up with EVA and glass, then laminated into sealed modules.

03

EL testing & framing

Each panel is EL-tested for micro-cracks, framed and fitted with a junction box.

04

Quality control

Final flash test, visual inspection and packing before export.
