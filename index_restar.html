About

Built for how Cameroon actually uses power

Restar Solar helps homes and businesses across Cameroon stay powered when the grid can't. We supply and install solar panels, lithium and tubular batteries, inverters, charge controllers, and complete off-grid kits — choosing equipment that holds up to heat, dust, and daily use.

Whether you need backup for a few lights and a fridge or a full system for a shop or borehole, we size it to your real needs, deliver it, and stay reachable afterwards.
